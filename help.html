How can I help?

Some of these critters are endangered, and some just need a little help some times. Here we will find out how kids and parents can help by volunteering or donating. Also there will be instructions and suggestions for what to do when finding an injured animal.

Injured critters

Kids: please tell an adult immediately and do not attempt to approach the animal! See below for phone numbers of local agencies that can assist:

  • Second Chances Wildlife Center - 502-999-9999
  • Local animal control
