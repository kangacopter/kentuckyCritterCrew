Hello there!

Welcome to the Kentucky Critter Crew website! This is a place for parents and kids to have fun learning about the local wildlife in Kentucky. Our mission is to bring awareness to the critters around us, their contribution to our lives, and how to help those critters. We also want those of all ages to have fun learning!

Meet the crew

Check out the Kentucky Critter Crew members: Lucy the skunk, Herman the Eastern red bat, Banjoanne the opossum, and Marko the raccoon!
